Built with Unity 6000.2.6f2
Can use left or right arrows to orbit the main camera in a circle around the look at position
Small camera attaches and shows the view from the current scene node in the dropdown menu, and follows any rotations it goes through

Rotating shapes are adjustable in Editor mode, and rotate around a path during play mode
Second drop down menu allows user to select primitives and change their color
Can use left and right (or a and d) keys to have the camera orbit around the figure
